# MKV to MP4 with OBS
Go to File, Remux Recordings, then drag and drop the file onto the window. Click Remux, and then your new MP4 will be saved to your OBS videos folder.[^1]

You can open MKV files with Elmedia Player.[^2]

[^1]: [How to Convert MKV to MP4 Free in OBS Studio | Easy! - YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O2c65LEf-6o)
[^2]: [How to open MKV files on Mac for free! - YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1gXAIZj4luQ)
