# Prep your kick transient for the limiter
Boost the knock (see [[Kick drum EQ guide]]).

Use a clipper to cut off just the pokiest transients. MDN uses [[StandardCLIP]].

Add a sample for low end resonance. Cut off the transient from the sample.[^1]
